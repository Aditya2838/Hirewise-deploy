Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data. Our goal is to ensure a secure and transparent experience for all users.

By using HIREWISE, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this policy. We strive to provide a trustworthy environment where job seekers and employers can connect without compromising their data security.

We continuously update our privacy measures to comply with evolving data protection laws and industry standards. Our commitment is to maintain transparency and empower users with control over their personal information.
